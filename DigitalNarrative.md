## Reverse Engineering

Learning from other people's examples is a time-effective and reliable method. I applied this technique to great effect when learning two technologies: HTML/CSS and DITA. For website creation using HTML and CSS, it was difficult to envision a polished website when I didn't know all the capabilities of those two languages. I could rely on my basic knowledge of different elements, but this wouldn't create a webpage that is rich in information and structure. So, with curiosity, I visited other websites to look at how they arranged different types of information with elements, and what relationships between specific parent and child elements existed.
